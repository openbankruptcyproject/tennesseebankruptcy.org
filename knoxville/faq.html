Knoxville Bankruptcy FAQ

Answers to common questions about filing bankruptcy in Knoxville.

Where do I file in Knoxville?

Eastern District of Tennessee, 800 Market St, Suite 330, Knoxville, TN 37902. Phone: (865) 545-4279.

How much does bankruptcy cost?

Chapter 7: $1,238-$2,188. Chapter 13: $2,838-$4,363.

State or federal exemptions?

Tennessee lets you choose. Federal homestead ($27,900) is usually better for homeowners. Details.

How long does bankruptcy take?

Chapter 7: 3-4 months. Chapter 13: 3-5 years.

What is the 341 meeting?

Brief hearing with the trustee ~30 days after filing. Learn more.

Can I file again?

Yes, with timing rules. Check eligibility.
