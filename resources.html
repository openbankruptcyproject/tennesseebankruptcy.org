Credit Counseling and Debtor Education

Two courses are required for bankruptcy: a pre-filing credit counseling course and a post-filing debtor education course. You must use agencies approved by the U.S. Trustee Program.

  • Approved agencies: justice.gov - approved agencies
  • Cost: $15-25 per course (reduced fees available)
  • Format: Online, phone, or in-person
  • Time: About 60-90 minutes per course

U.S. Trustee Program

The U.S. Trustee Program oversees the administration of bankruptcy cases.

  • Region 8: (901) 544-3251
  • Website: justice.gov/ust

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the bankruptcy court in Tennessee?

Tennessee has 3 federal bankruptcy courts. W.D. Tenn.: 200 Jefferson Ave, Suite 413, Memphis, TN 38103 ((901) 328-3500); M.D. Tenn.: 701 Broadway, Nashville, TN 37203 ((615) 736-5584); E.D. Tenn.: 800 Market St, Suite 330, Knoxville, TN 37902 ((865) 545-4279)

Is there free legal help for bankruptcy in Tennessee?

Yes. Legal Aid Society of Middle Tennessee ((615) 244-6610) provides free legal services. Memphis Area Legal Services ((901) 523-8822) provides free legal services. Legal Aid of East Tennessee ((865) 637-0484) provides free legal services.

Where can I take the required credit counseling course?

The U.S. Trustee Program maintains a list of approved credit counseling agencies at justice.gov/ust. Most offer courses online or by phone for $15-25.
