Chapter 7 vs. Chapter 13: Which Is Right for You?

Chapter 7 - Fresh Start

Eliminates most unsecured debts in 3-4 months. Must pass the means test.

  • Discharge in 3-4 months
  • Eliminates credit cards, medical debt
  • Filing fee: $338
Learn more about Chapter 7 →

Chapter 13 - Payment Plan

Keep your property while repaying debts over 3-5 years.

  • 3-5 year repayment plan
  • Keep your home and car
  • Filing fee: $313
Learn more about Chapter 13 →

How Much Does Bankruptcy Cost in Nashville?

  • Chapter 7 filing fee: $338
  • Chapter 13 filing fee: $313
  • Attorney fees: $1,000-$2,000 for Chapter 7; $2,500-$4,500 for Chapter 13
  • Credit counseling: $25-$50 total

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I lose my house?

Middle District of Tennessee, 701 Broadway, Nashville, TN 37203. Phone: (615) 736-5584.

How long does bankruptcy take?

Chapter 7: 3-4 months. Chapter 13: 3-5 years. 341 meeting about 30 days after filing.
