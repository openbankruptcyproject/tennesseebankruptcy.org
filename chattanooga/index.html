Chapter 7 vs. Chapter 13

Chapter 7 - Fresh Start

Eliminates most unsecured debts in 3-4 months. Must pass the means test. Filing fee: $338.

Chapter 7 guide →

Chapter 13 - Payment Plan

Keep property, repay over 3-5 years. No means test. Filing fee: $313.
