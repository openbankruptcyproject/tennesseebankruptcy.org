Memphis Bankruptcy Filing Checklist

Use this checklist to prepare for your Memphis bankruptcy filing. Proper preparation helps ensure a smooth process.

Documents to Gather

  • Last 6 months of pay stubs or income records
  • Last 2 years of federal and state tax returns
  • Bank statements (all accounts, last 3-6 months)
  • Mortgage statements and property tax bills
  • Vehicle titles and loan statements
  • Credit card and loan statements (all debts)
  • Medical bills and collection letters
  • Insurance policies
  • Retirement account statements (401k, IRA)
  • Any court judgments, garnishment orders, or lawsuits
  • Lease agreements
  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Social Security card

Steps Before Filing

1Complete credit counseling -- From an approved provider (within 180 days of filing)
2Decide: state or federal exemptions -- Compare to protect the most property. Exemption guide
3Run the means test -- Determine Chapter 7 eligibility. Means test guide
4List ALL debts -- Missing a debt means it may not be discharged
5List ALL assets -- Hiding assets is a federal crime
6Stop using credit cards -- Charges near filing time may be considered fraud
7Do not transfer property -- Transfers within 2 years can be reversed

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Paying back family members before filing (preferential transfers)
  • Running up credit card debt before filing
  • Transferring property to friends or family
  • Cashing out retirement accounts (they are exempt!)
  • Filing without comparing state vs. federal exemptions
  • Not listing all creditors and debts
