How Chapter 13 Works

In Chapter 13, you propose a repayment plan to pay back some or all of your debts over 3-5 years. You make monthly payments to a trustee, who distributes funds to creditors. At the end, remaining eligible unsecured debts are discharged.

Chapter 13 is especially useful for:

  • Saving your home -- catch up on missed mortgage payments over the plan
  • Keeping your car -- cure defaults and potentially reduce the loan balance
  • Stopping wage garnishments -- the automatic stay halts collections
  • Repaying tax debts -- spread tax obligations over 3-5 years

The Memphis Dismissal Problem

W.D. Tenn. has a 79.6% Chapter 13 dismissal rate, making it the worst-performing district in the country. This means fewer than 1 in 4 Chapter 13 filers here actually receive a discharge.

The 53.5% prior-filer rate indicates that many people in this district file Chapter 13 multiple times without ever completing a plan. Each filing costs money and time while providing only temporary relief from the automatic stay.

Before filing Chapter 13 in Memphis, seriously consider whether Chapter 7 is an option. If you qualify for Chapter 7, it eliminates debt in months rather than years and does not depend on completing a multi-year plan.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Chapter 13 dismissal rate in Memphis?

79.6% -- the worst in the nation. More than 3 out of 4 Chapter 13 cases in the Western District of Tennessee end in dismissal rather than discharge.

Can I save my house from foreclosure in Chapter 13?

Yes. The automatic stay stops foreclosure immediately, and your plan can include catch-up payments on the mortgage arrearage. More details.

What happens if my Chapter 13 is dismissed?

Dismissal means creditors can resume collection activity. You lose the protection of the automatic stay. Repeated filings may result in a shortened or denied stay. Learn more about dismissal.

Should I file Chapter 7 instead?

Given the 79.6% dismissal rate, Chapter 7 may be the safer option if you qualify. Compare Chapter 7 vs 13.
